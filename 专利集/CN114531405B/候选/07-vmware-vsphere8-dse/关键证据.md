| 项 | 内容 |
|---|---|

| 网页链接 | [DSE Install (HA quote)](https://techdocs.broadcom.com/us/en/vmware-cis/vsphere/vsphere/8-0/esx-installation-and-setup/introducing-vmware-vsphere-distributed-services-engine-and-networking-acceleration-by-using-dpus-install.html) ; [Network Offloads Compatibility (Non-HA quote)](https://techdocs.broadcom.com/us/en/vmware-cis/vsphere/vsphere/8-0/vsphere-networking/basic-networking-with-vnetwork-distributed-switches/network-offloads-capability.html) |
